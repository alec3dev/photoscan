Why Is The Camera Mirrored?

Most camera applications show a mirrored view (horizontally flipped) by default. This is done intentionally to provide a more natural experience:

Non-Mirrored (How others see you)

Mirrored (How you see yourself in a mirror)

When you raise your right hand in a mirrored view, it appears on the right side of the screen, which matches your mental model of how mirrors work. This feels more intuitive for selfie-taking and video calls.

However, when the final photo is captured, it's typically saved in its non-mirrored form (how others would see you) to maintain consistency with traditional photography.
